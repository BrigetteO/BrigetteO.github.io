Cold Brew

A summertime favorite...

I distinctly remember my first cold brew coffee (with coconut milk, of course). I was sitting in the sun at Pannikin after a long run with friends. Nothing had ever tasted so delicious. The benefits of cold brew include lower acidity and higher caffeine content.

How it's made

  1. Grind 3/4 cup of beans very coarsely per 4 cups of water
  2. Soak the coarse-ground beans in room temperature (or cold) water for a very long time, usually 12 hours or more
  3. Filter the grounds out of the water using a paper coffee filter, a fine metal sieve, a French press or felt
